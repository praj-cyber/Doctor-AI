about::

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). 
It works by reducing hormones that cause inflammation and pain in the body. 
Ibuprofen is used to reduce fever and treat pain
 or inflammation caused by many conditions such as headache, 
toothache, back pain, arthritis, menstrual cramps, or minor injury.
