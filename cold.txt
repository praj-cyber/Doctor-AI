problem : cold
				 
				medicine: guaifenesin,dextromethorphan
				use     : two times a day


about::
Cough suppressants , like dextromethorphan, can provide relief for a short time. 
They work on the part of your brain that controls the process.
Expectorants, like guaifenesin, can break up congestion in your chest by thinning the mucus in your airways.
